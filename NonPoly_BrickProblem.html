The Brick Problem

What is the Brick Problem?

The Brick Problem is a game where a tower of brick are created and given random values. Two players take turns pulling either one or two bricks per turn. Each brick that the player pulls adds to their respective score. When there are no more bricks left, the player with the higher score wins!
