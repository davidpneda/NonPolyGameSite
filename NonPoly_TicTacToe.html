3D Tic Tac Toe

What is 3D Tic-Tac-Toe?

The 3D Tic-Tac-Toe game is a spin on the original, and more commonly seen, Tic-Tac-Toe. Where the original Tic-Tac-Toe utilizes a 3 by 3 grid on a two-dimensional plane, our game uses a 3 by 3 by 3 space within a three-dimensional area.

How do you win 3D Tic-Tac-Toe?

There are multiple ways to win the 3D Tic-Tac-Toe game. Some more obvious than others:

  • One may win by selecting three spaces across any of the area's faces (Front, back, left, right, top, bottom).
  • Another way to win is by selecting three spacecs in a downward/upward direction on any of the area's faces.
  • One more way to win the game is to select three spaces in a diagonal across any of the area's faces.
  • Lastly, one may win by selecting three spaces within the 3 by 3 by 3 area that follow the previously stated patterns.
